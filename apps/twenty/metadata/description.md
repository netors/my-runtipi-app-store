Twenty is a modern, open-source CRM platform designed to be a refreshing alternative to Salesforce.

## Features

- **Contact & Company Management**: Track all your contacts, companies, and opportunities in one place
- **Pipeline Management**: Visualize and manage your sales pipeline with a Kanban board
- **Task Management**: Create and assign tasks, set reminders, and track progress
- **Email Integration**: Connect your email to track conversations and communications
- **Notes & Activities**: Log all interactions and maintain a complete activity timeline
- **Custom Fields**: Extend the data model with custom fields to fit your workflow
- **API & Webhooks**: Full REST and GraphQL API for integrations
- **Self-hosted**: Full control over your data with self-hosted deployment

## System Requirements

- Minimum 2GB RAM recommended
- PostgreSQL database (included)
- Redis for caching and background jobs (included)

## Getting Started

After installation, access Twenty at the configured domain. The first user to sign up will be the workspace administrator.
